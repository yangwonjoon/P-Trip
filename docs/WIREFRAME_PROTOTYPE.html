Gwangjang Market

광장시장

street-food traditional budget instagram

One of Seoul's oldest and largest traditional markets, dating back to 1905. Famous for bindaetteok (mung bean pancakes), mayak gimbap, and fresh knife-cut noodles.

Come hungry — this is where locals eat.

🕓

Hours

9:00 AM - 11:00 PM (closed Sundays)

📍

Address

88 Changgyeonggung-ro, Jongno-gu, Seoul

🚆

Nearest station

Jongno 5-ga (Line 1) — 2 min walk

💰

Budget

5,000 - 15,000 KRW per person
